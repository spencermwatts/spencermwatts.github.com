How do you make a hiring decision as easy as ?

Summary

subject an app for employers actively hiring employees

goal help managers select the right candidates quickly

solution relevant information, helpful visual queues

scope hypothetical improvements on an existing app

Inform product design with user feedback and research

We know from user research that managers care about dependability, so let's make that part of the experience.

Dependability breaks down into two parts:

1. distance - the closer the jobseeker, the more likely they are to show up for shifts.

2. previous roles. it matters more what the jobseeker has done, not where they've done it.

2. Don't leave them hanging; remind them of exactly what they were searching

3. Let them dig a little deeper to better understand the candidate and his/her skills
